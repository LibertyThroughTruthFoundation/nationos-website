## STEP-BY-STEP SETUP GUIDE

### Step 1: Create Your Donation Wallet

**IMPORTANT:** Create a **brand new wallet** just for donations. Do NOT use an existing wallet with other funds.

**Instructions:**

1. Install MetaMask browser extension
2. Click "Create a new wallet"
3. Create a strong password
4. **CRITICAL:** Write down your 12-word "seed phrase" on paper and store it safely
   - This is the ONLY way to recover your wallet if you lose access
   - NEVER share this with anyone
   - NEVER store it digitally (no screenshots, no cloud storage)
5. Confirm your seed phrase
6. Your wallet is created!

**Your Wallet Address:**

After setup, you'll see your wallet address (starts with `0x...`). This is like your account number. You'll share this publicly for donations.

Example: `0x742d35Cc6634C0532925a3b844Bc9e7595f0bEb`

### Step 2: Add PulseChain Network to MetaMask

1. Open MetaMask
2. Click the network dropdown (top center, probably says "Ethereum Mainnet")
3. Click "Add Network"
4. Click "Add a network manually"
5. Enter the PulseChain network details (from above)
6. Click "Save"
7. Switch to PulseChain network

### Step 3: Get PLS for Gas Fees

You need a small amount of PLS to pay for transaction fees.

**Option A: Buy on an Exchange**
- Some exchanges support PulseChain (check current listings)
- Withdraw PLS directly to your wallet address

**Option B: Use a Bridge**
- Bridge ETH or other tokens from Ethereum to PulseChain
- Use a service like PulseChain Bridge

**Option C: Ask for Help**
- If you know someone in the crypto space, ask them to send you $10 worth of PLS
- Give them your wallet address

### Step 4: Get USDC.e (Optional for Testing)

You don't need USDC.e to receive donations, but it's helpful to test the system.

1. Go to PulseX: https://pulsex.com
2. Connect your MetaMask wallet
3. Swap a small amount of PLS for USDC.e
4. Confirm the transaction

### Step 5: Create Your Donation Page

Use the HTML template provided in this kit (see `donation-page-template.html`).

**Customize the template:**

1. Replace `0xYourDonationWalletAddressHere` with your actual wallet address
2. Add your name/ministry/research focus
3. Optionally add a photo or logo
4. Save the file as `support.html`

**Where to Host It:**

- **Option A:** Add it to your existing website
- **Option B:** Use GitHub Pages (free hosting)
- **Option C:** Use a simple web host (Netlify, Vercel - both free)

### Step 6: Share Your Donation Address

Once your page is live, share it with your audience:

- Add a link in your YouTube video descriptions
- Pin a post on your social media
- Mention it in your content
- Add it to your email signature

**Example Announcement:**

> "I've set up a new way to support this work that can't be censored or frozen by Big Tech. If you'd like to contribute, visit [your link] for details. Thank you for helping keep this research independent!"

## RECEIVING & MANAGING DONATIONS

### How to Check Your Balance

1. Open MetaMask
2. Make sure you're on PulseChain network
3. Click "Assets"
4. You'll see your PLS balance and any USDC.e you've received

### How to Convert to Cash

When you want to convert your crypto donations to USD:

**Option A: Sell on an Exchange**
1. Send your USDC.e to a centralized exchange (Coinbase, Kraken, etc.)
2. Sell USDC.e for USD
3. Withdraw USD to your bank account

**Option B: Peer-to-Peer**
1. Find a trusted buyer in your network
2. Send them USDC.e
3. They send you cash/check/Venmo

**Option C: Crypto Debit Card**
1. Get a crypto debit card (BitPay, Crypto.com)
2. Load your USDC.e onto the card
3. Spend directly like a normal debit card

### Security Best Practices

1. **Keep your seed phrase offline** - Write it on paper, store in a safe
2. **Use a hardware wallet for large amounts** - Ledger or Trezor
3. **Never share your seed phrase** - Not even with "support" people
4. **Test with small amounts first** - Send yourself a test donation
5. **Keep your computer secure** - Antivirus, updated software, no sketchy downloads
