Why Cryptocurrency?

Cryptocurrency provides a censorship-resistant alternative. It's not about speculation or "getting rich"—it's about financial sovereignty and the ability to support independent work without asking permission from hostile corporations.

Privacy Note: Cryptocurrency transactions are pseudonymous. You don't need to reveal your identity to donate. However, transactions are recorded on a public blockchain.
