Two Paths to Participation

Whether you're ready to commit as a covenant member or called to build the infrastructure as a technical co-founder, we welcome those who resonate with the vision and are ready to make it real.

Become a Covenant Member

What is Covenant Membership?

Covenant membership is not a mere subscription or transaction. It is a solemn commitment - a vertical covenant with God and a horizontal covenant with fellow members.

Requirements

  • Public Assent: Voluntary agreement to The Sovereign Way Constitution and its principles
  • Faith Commitment: Confession of Jesus Christ as Lord and Savior
  • Economic Participation: Tangible commitment of resources demonstrating skin in the game
  • Community Engagement: Active participation in covenant community life

What You Receive

  • Access to covenant community resources and infrastructure
  • Participation in governance according to covenant tier
  • Protection and support from the covenant community
  • Economic participation in covenant prosperity
  • Chi Rho Passport NFT (covenant identity marker)
  • Access to learning pods, third place spaces, and community events

Your Responsibilities

  • Act with integrity and uphold the constitution
  • Assist fellow covenant members in need
  • Submit to biblically-based dispute resolution
  • Contribute to the community according to ability and calling

Become a Builder (Technical Co-Founder)

We Need Covenant Partners, Not Employees

We are seeking technical co-founders who will help build the NationOS infrastructure. This is not a job posting; this is a call to partnership. We need builders with skin in the game - covenant partners who share the vision and are ready to commit.

What We're Building

  • Covenant Finance (CoFi): JUBILEE token, pCUSD stablecoin, vaults, staking contracts
  • Chi Rho Passport: Self-sovereign identity NFT system
  • GodManMarkets.pls: User-owned marketplace platform
  • Covenant Courier: Privacy-respecting logistics infrastructure
  • Decentralized Communication: Uncensorable media and messaging
  • And more... All 12 Pillars need builders

Technical Skills We Need

  • Blockchain development (Solidity, smart contracts, PulseChain/EVM)
  • Full-stack web development (React, Node.js, databases)
  • Decentralized systems (IPFS, Arweave, peer-to-peer protocols)
  • Security and cryptography
  • DevOps and infrastructure
  • UI/UX design with covenant values

What We Offer

  • Covenant Partnership: Equity/token allocation, not just salary
  • Meaningful Work: Building Kingdom infrastructure, not just another app
  • Autonomy: Trusted to build according to the vision
  • Community: Working alongside covenant members who share your values
  • Legacy: Multi-generational impact, not short-term extraction

What We Require

  • Faith: Confession of Jesus Christ as Lord
  • Alignment: Agreement with The Sovereign Way Constitution
  • Skill: Demonstrated technical ability in relevant areas
  • Commitment: Ready to build for the long term, not just a gig
  • Integrity: Character matters more than credentials
