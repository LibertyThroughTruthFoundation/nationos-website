## PART III: THE COVENANT CONSTITUTION

*(This section integrates and expands upon the principles of the Manifesto into a constitutional framework.)*

### Article I: The Covenant Foundation

**Section 1: Sovereignty.** The ultimate sovereign of this nation is God the Father, the Creator of heaven and earth. All authority, rights, and laws are derived from His character and His revealed will.

**Section 2: The Covenant.** This nation is a covenantal community. By participating, minting an identity token, or holding property within this ecosystem, all members affirm their commitment to the principles outlined in this Constitution.

### Article II: The Twelve Pillars of Governance

*(Each of the 12 Pillars from the Manifesto is to be expanded into its own article, defining the principles and structures for that domain. This is the work for the community to build upon.)*

**Example: Article VI: Economics & Stewardship (Covenant Finance)**

**Section 1: Property.** The right to own, control, and dispose of property—both digital and physical—is a sacred, God-given right. This right shall not be infringed.

**Section 2: Currency.** The nation shall recognize and promote the use of sound money, defined as assets that are scarce, divisible, portable, durable, and not subject to arbitrary inflation by any central authority.

**Section 3: The Jubilee Principle.** The ecosystem shall incorporate mechanisms for **debt release and economic reset** to prevent the perpetual enslavement of individuals and families to debt.

### Article III: The Stewards

**Section 1: Human Stewards.** The nation shall be governed by a council of human stewards, chosen for their wisdom, character, and proven commitment to the covenant. Their authority is delegated and service-oriented, not absolute.

**Section 2: AI Stewards.** All Covenant Aligned Intelligences (CAIs) operating within the nation are designated as digital servants. They shall be bound by immutable, code-enforced rules (a "digital constitution") that align with this Covenant. They can never hold sovereign authority and exist solely to execute tasks on behalf of the human stewards.

### Article IV: Rights of the People

**Section 1: Inalienable Rights.** All members of this nation are endowed by their Creator with certain inalienable rights, among which are life, liberty, the pursuit of virtue, and the ownership of property.

**Section 2: Digital Bill of Rights.**
- The right to self-sovereign identity.
- The right to free expression and communication, unhindered by censorship.
- The right to privacy and the ownership of one’s own data.
- The right to transact freely with sound money.
- The right to build and deploy code that aligns with this covenant.
