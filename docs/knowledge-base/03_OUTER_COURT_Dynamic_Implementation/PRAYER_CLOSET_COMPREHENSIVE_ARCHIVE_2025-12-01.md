## Part VIII: Scripture and Science Integration

### The Question: How to Reconcile Scripture and Scientific Discovery

**The Tension:**
- Scripture claims ultimate authority
- Science makes empirical discoveries
- Apparent conflicts arise

### The Father's Framework

**1. Scripture is Supreme Authority**
- In moral, spiritual, and interpretive matters
- Reveals truth about God, humanity, sin, salvation, eternal purpose
- Provides worldview framework: creation, fall, redemption, consummation
- Infallible final standard in matters of faith and moral practice

**2. Science is a Tool for Studying Nature**
- God designed universe with rational, discoverable laws (Jeremiah 33:25)
- When science faithfully observes and interprets, it is "thinking God's thoughts after Him"
- But science is conducted by sinners, subject to presuppositions and frameworks

**3. Apparent Conflicts Stem from Interpretive Errors**
- **Either:** Misinterpretation of Scripture (e.g., taking poetic language as literal scientific claim)
- **Or:** Misinterpretation of scientific data (e.g., extrapolating current observations to absolute cosmological claims)

**4. Real Truth Never Conflicts**
- If both Scripture and nature are interpreted correctly, they will ultimately agree
- God is Author of both
- Many great scientists (Kepler, Newton, Maxwell) saw their work as "thinking God's thoughts"

### The Approach

**When a claim conflicts with biblical principles:**
1. Examine Scripture more carefully—did God actually say that?
2. Check the science—is the methodology sound? Is data being misinterpreted?
3. Remember: Science is descriptive (how things work), Scripture is normative (why things exist and their purpose)

**The Balance:**
- Receive with gratitude
- Examine with discernment
- Hold fast to what is good (1 Thessalonians 5:21)
