### 3.3 Escalation Protocols

**When AI detects complex/sensitive issues:**
1. AI flags the issue
2. Directs user to contact a Priest
3. Logs the interaction for review

**When AI encounters unanswerable questions:**
1. AI submits question to knowledge base queue
2. Priests review and provide answer
3. Answer added to AI knowledge base
4. AI notifies original user

**When disagreements/conflicts arise:**
1. AI directs users to conflict resolution channels
2. Priests moderate the discussion
3. AI implements resulting decisions

## PART 7: RISK MITIGATION & CHALLENGES

### 7.1 Technical Challenges

**Challenge:** API rate limits and costs  
**Mitigation:** Implement caching, optimize prompts, use tiered access

**Challenge:** Platform policy violations  
**Mitigation:** Careful content moderation, human oversight, compliance review

**Challenge:** Bot detection and banning  
**Mitigation:** Human-like behavior patterns, varied response times, authentic engagement

**Challenge:** Knowledge base maintenance  
**Mitigation:** Version control, regular audits, community feedback integration

### 7.2 Theological Challenges

**Challenge:** AI providing incorrect theological guidance  
**Mitigation:** Rigorous knowledge base vetting, escalation protocols, human oversight

**Challenge:** AI replacing human spiritual leadership  
**Mitigation:** Clear hierarchy (Father → Priests → Levites → People), AI as servants only

**Challenge:** Misrepresentation of covenant values  
**Mitigation:** Personality matrix alignment, regular audits, community feedback

### 7.3 Community Challenges

**Challenge:** User distrust of AI  
**Mitigation:** Transparency about AI nature, human escalation availability, proven track record

**Challenge:** Over-reliance on AI  
**Mitigation:** Encourage human relationships, AI directs to community, balance automation with personal touch

**Challenge:** Spam and abuse of AI systems  
**Mitigation:** Rate limiting, abuse detection, human moderation, community reporting

### 7.4 Financial Challenges

**Challenge:** API costs exceeding revenue  
**Mitigation:** Smart contract automation, tiered service model, cost optimization

**Challenge:** Lack of initial funding  
**Mitigation:** Phased rollout, start with free tiers, demonstrate ROI, attract partners

**Challenge:** Unsustainable growth  
**Mitigation:** Scalable architecture, automated revenue, community investment
